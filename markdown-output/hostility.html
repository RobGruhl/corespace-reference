Hostility Tracker Overview

The hostility tracker is a doom clock measuring enemy awareness. As it fills, enemies become more numerous and dangerous.

Increases Each Round: +1 peg (or +2 with 4-6 crews)
First Shot Penalty: First ranged assault each round adds +1 peg
Rarely Decreases: Some Tech skills (Regulate) or special effects

Event Cards

Drawing & Resolving

  1. First player draws from event deck in Hostility Phase
  2. Look at current hostility level
  3. Resolve effects for that level ONLY
  4. Discard to faceup pile

Assistance Cards (üëç)

Special: Keep secret, don't show other players
Use: Discard during your turn for benefit

Unresolvable Cards

  • Resolve what you can
  • If completely unresolvable + has NPC icon: Place NPC instead
  • Shuffle card back, draw another

Strategic Management

Increasing Hostility

  • Automatic: +1 every round
  • First shot: +1 from ammo peg
  • Event cards: Some add pegs
  • Terrain effects: Varies

Strategy Tips

Work Fast: Complete objectives before CLEANSE
Coordinate Shooting: Agree who shoots first each round
Plan Extraction: Know your escape route before hostility = 20
