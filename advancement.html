Skills Overview

Cost: Skill pegs = level chosen (1-3)
Limit: ONE skill use per round (or spend 1 peg for 2nd effortless action)
Permanent Cost: Spent pegs stay removed entire game

Skill Types

Passive
Always on, no peg cost
Active
Use during your turn
Reaction
Trigger outside your turn

Key Skills by Class

Tech Class

Accelerate
Active skill
Level 1: Make Move action
Level 2: 2 extra Move/Assault actions
Level 3: 3 extra Move/Assault actions
Regulate
Active skill - hostility control
Level 1: Remove hostility peg OR 1 repair point
Level 2: Turn over entry point (First Born won't use)
Level 3: Choose point, all First Born in medium range move there, don't act
Hack
Active skill - tech warfare
Level 1: Shut down rank 1-2 First Born OR lock/unlock door
Level 2: Shut down 2 rank 1-2 First Born
Level 3: As level 2 OR control rank 1-3 OR lock/unlock any door
Assist
Active skill - team support
Level 1: Target can't be harmed by ranged this round
Level 2: Target restores 2 health
Level 3: 3 characters each make 1 action

Ranged Class

Sure Shot
Active skill
Level 1: Ranged at short, 1 auto-hit (ignore cover), can't move
Level 2: Ranged at medium even after moving, 1 auto-hit
Level 3: Medium range after moving, 1 auto-hit (ignore cover AND armour)
Marksman
Active skill - multi-target
Level 1: Ranged attack
Level 2: +1 die, split hits between 2 targets in short range
Level 3: +2 dice, split hits between any targets in short range
Counter Shot
Reaction skill
Trigger: After being targeted with ranged attack
Level 1: Ranged attack against attacker
Level 2: At any time after enemy's 1st action, ranged attack
Level 3: At any time, ranged (+1 die) + move
Weapons Expert
Passive + Active
Passive 1: Reload as effortless
Level 1: Ranged +2 dice, can't move
Passive 2: Pistol assault as effortless
Level 2: Fire 2 pistols as 1 action (add dice -1)
Level 3: Shoot weapon from enemy's hand (2 dice, no damage, scatter)
Disarm
Reaction skill
Trigger: After enemy close assault where you took no damage
Level 1: Remove/scatter their weapon
Level 2: Steal their weapon
Level 3: As level 2 + close assault + move (no attacks of opportunity)

Passive 1: Ignore 1 hit in attacks of opportunity

Reaction 1: Ignore effect losing actions/missing turn

Level 1: Base contact with wall, can't be targeted
Level 2: Ranged assault + base contact with terrain, can't be targeted

Campaign System

Post-Game Sequence

  1. Extraction Phase - Rescue defeated traders
  2. Advancement Phase - Gain career points
  3. Trade Phase - Buy/sell equipment
  4. Maintenance Phase - Repair ship

Extraction Phase

If all traders made it out: Skip this phase

For each defeated trader:

  1. Roll chance die
  2. üíÄ result = Permanently dead (remove from campaign)
  3. Otherwise, choose option:
    • Rescue Mission: Play immediate mission on same board
    • Emergency Teleport: Roll on table for result
    • On Your Own: Roll on table if no crew aboard

Advancement Phase

Career Points: Each trader that survived AND made it to ship gains +1 career point
True Born Kill: Exchange counter for +1 additional point

Spending Career Points

  • Fill next empty space on trader board
  • Can also allocate to skills on class board

Leveling Up

LevelRewards
Level 1+1 Health, +1 Skill (permanent)
Level 2+1 Health, +1 Skill (permanent)
Level 3+1 Health, +1 Skill, +1 Action (permanent, max level)

Trade Phase

Each Crew Chooses ONE Trading Post

  • Buy items: Pay cost (first number on token)
  • Sell items: Receive value (second number on token)
  • Rare items (üíé): Cannot be bought
  • Objective tokens with UA: Can be sold
  • Stock limited to available tokens
  • Can barter with other players

Hiring Crew

  • Can hire new traders (max 7 total)
  • Pay cost shown

Permanent NPC Hires

If NPC joined crew during mission AND made it to ship:
  • Can permanently hire for cost shown
  • Becomes trader for future missions
  • Exception: Galactic Corps and Security cannot hire

Maintenance Phase

Ship Systems

  1. Degradation Check: Roll chance die, apply to ship system
  2. Repairs: 2 UA per repair point (fill 1 space)
  3. Upgrades: Purchase ship upgrades (Scanners, Auto-Defences, Docking Thrusters - 3 levels each)
If any system reaches 0: Ship destroyed, crew out of campaign (unless repair this phase)
