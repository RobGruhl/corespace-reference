Actions Overview

Traders can take a number of actions equal to their ACTION stat (usually 2-4). Choose from the actions below. Some actions are "effortless" and don't count toward this limit.

Restrictions: Prone characters can only Stand Up. Defeated characters cannot act at all.

Movement Actions

Move
1 Action
Requirements: None
Effect: Move up to 4" (or 4 squares if gridded)
Rules:
  • Measure from edge of base
  • Can move any direction
  • Can move through friendly characters
  • Cannot move through enemies without permission
  • Cannot move through/over terrain (unless Scramble ability)
  • MAX 11" per round across all moves
‚ö†Ô∏è Attack of Opportunity: Leaving base contact with enemy = they get free Close Assault
Proximity Move
Effortless
Requirements: None
Effect: Move up to 1"
Note: Still counts toward 11" maximum per round

Combat Actions

Ranged Assault
1 Action
Requirements: Target in LoS & range, not engaged with target, have ammo

  2. Measure range (Short 0-5" / Medium 5-13" / Long 13"+)

First Shot Each Round: Place ammo peg in hostility tracker (not discard pile)
Engaged with Different Enemy? Can shoot, but engaged enemy makes free Close Assault first
Shooting into Melee: Short range = pick target | Med/Long = roll chance die after hits

Utility Actions

Search
1 Action
Requirements: See types below, not engaged
Search Terrain:
  • In base contact with searchable terrain (crate, ark, stasis pod)
  • Open, take contents (keep secret)
  • Add items to dashboard or discard back
  • Can search same terrain multiple times
General Search:
  • In a room (enclosed by walls/board edge)
  • No enemies in room
  • Draw 1 random standard item from token pouch
  • Place Search Counter (once per room only)
Mine
1 Action
Requirements: Have mining tool, adjacent to solid wall, not engaged
Steps:
  1. Place Mine Counter in room
  2. Draw 1 random small token
  3. Add to dashboard or discard

Special Mechanics

Attacks of Opportunity

Trigger: Character leaves base contact with 1+ standing enemies
Effect: ALL engaged standing enemies make free Close Assault
Timing: Resolves immediately (interrupts movement)
Note: Doesn't use attacker's actions, can be done even if already activated

Entering/Leaving Ship

First action of game: Must be Move to enter board from airlock
Return to ship: Move to airlock (need 1" remaining to enter)
Once left board: Out of game, cannot return
Enemy access: Cannot enter your ship without permission
