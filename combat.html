Line of Sight (LoS)

Drawing LoS

Draw straight line from center of your base to any part of target

Can Draw LoS If:

  • ‚úì Not completely blocked by wall
  • ‚úì Not completely blocked by terrain as tall as target
  • ‚úì Not completely blocked by another character

Character Height

  • All characters considered same height
  • Entire area above base blocks LoS
  • Exception: Massive üèîÔ∏è characters can be seen past smaller ones

Cover System

Determining Cover

Measure how much of target is obscured by terrain/walls:

Obscured Cover Level Effect
< 25% No Cover No modifier
25-90% Partial Cover -1 hit
> 90% Full Cover Cannot draw LoS

Cover Rules

  • Only applies to ranged attacks
  • Does NOT apply to close assault
  • Does NOT apply to attacks without origin (card effects, etc.)
  • Applied before armour
  • Prone characters still affected by cover normally

Physical Armour

  • Reduces hits by value shown
  • Works on EVERY attack
  • Never depletes
  • Example: Armour 1 + 3 hits = 2 damage

Shield Armour

  • Reduces hits by value shown
  • If hits ‚â§ shield value: Blocks all, shield stays active
  • If hits > shield value: Blocks some, remaining damage, shield OVERLOADS
  • Overloaded: Flip facedown, inactive rest of mission
  • Recharges between missions (campaign)

Multiple Armour

If have both physical and shield:
  • Use highest value
  • If equal: Shield takes priority

Combat Armour

Some armour has unarmed combat icon:
  • Can use as Close Assault weapon
  • Roll dice shown
  • Icons don't stack (use one or the other)

Dyson Equipment vs Shield Armour

IMPORTANT: Dyson equipment does NOT work if wearing ANY shield armour (even if inactive/overloaded)

Damage Priority Order (from First Born energy attacks):

  1. Deflection items (Ecco Orb) - reflects attack back
  2. Cover (partial = -1 hit)
  3. Armour (physical OR shield - use highest value only)
  4. Dyson equipment (-1 hit, +1 energy counter) - ONLY if NOT wearing shield armour
  5. Shield pegs (from utility equipment)
  6. Remaining hits = damage to health
Note: Deflection and Dyson equipment only affect "First Born energy" attacks (ranged attacks from First Born or green equipment tokens). Regular attacks ignore these effects.

Damage Application

Against Traders

  • Lose 1 Health peg per damage
  • If Health reaches 0: Defeated
  • Lay figure on side
  • Place assistance counter
  • Cannot be targeted by enemies
  • Can be revived or dragged

Against NPCs

  • Unless stated otherwise: Instantly defeated if take any damage
  • Remove from play
  • Items drop on floor

Exceptions

True Born: Has 4 health inserts, not instantly defeated
Some NPCs: May have health track (check board)

Special Combat Situations

Shooting at Engaged Characters

Short range: Pick target freely (close enough to aim)
Med/Long range: After rolling hits, roll chance die to determine who you hit

Engaged While Shooting

Cannot shoot target you're engaged with
Can shoot other targets, but engaged enemy makes free Close Assault first
