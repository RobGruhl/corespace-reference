General Rules

  • "Cannot" beats "Must": Cannot always overrides permissive rules
  • Rounding: Always round up (unless specified)
  • Minimums: Cannot go below 0 (negative = 0)
  • Simultaneous effects: First player chooses order
  • Equal options: First player (or acting player) chooses
  • Re-rolls: Cannot re-roll unless specified
