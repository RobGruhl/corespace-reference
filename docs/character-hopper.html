Hopper's Skills

Machine Skills

Reroute
Active + Between Games
Level 1 (Between Games): After defeated: no longer defeated - restore 2 Health and Stand Up
Level 2: Activate self-repair systems - restore 2 Health
Level 3: When in base contact with wall (no door/window), patch into substructure to recharge - restore Health to maximum
Overdrive
Active skill
Level 1: Take any action
Level 2: Take any two actions, then lose 1 Health
Level 3: Self-destruct: Ranged attack 5 dice (short range) or 2 dice (medium range) to all characters. Roll Chance Die: 1-2 = destroyed permanently, 3-4 = destroyed but equipment scatters, 5-6 = AI chip survives as Objective token (can restore in Trade Phase)
Repair
Active skill
Level 1: Repair or Clear Jam action on self or any character/equipment in base contact, with +1 automatic hit
Level 2: Apply 3 Repair Points to self or any item/machine in base contact
Level 3: Release repair nanites: Fully repair one object/heal any Machine in short range. All other objects/items/Machines in short range apply 1 Repair Point
Bodyguard Protocol
Active + Between Games
Level 1 (Between Games): At any time, make Move action
Level 2: Target friendly character in base contact restores 1 Health. While in contact, may take any/all hits they suffer until end of round
Level 3 (Between Games): At any time: two Move actions and Close Assault (any order). After Close Assault, if enemy not defeated, roll die - 1+ hits knocks them prone
