  2. If all in cover: Closest visible
  3. If none visible: Closest (around walls)
  4. Assassins: Always Captain if visible

Rock Worms

Rank 3 Beast - Special Rules

  • Don't move, stay on worm hole
  • Attack range: 2 squares from hole
  • React to ANY action in range (NOT skills or effortless actions)
  • Attack Timing: Resolves AFTER the action completes
  • If entering attack range: Attack interrupts movement
  • Cannot be knocked prone
  • Immune to Fire and Poison
  • Don't alert Drones
  • First Born ignore Rock Worms (won't attack, avoid attack range)
Example: If you take a Move action while already in range, complete your full move first, then the worm attacks. But if you're entering the range from outside, the attack interrupts your movement.

True Born (Boss Enemy)

States

Somnambulant: Sleepwalking, scatter only
Awake: Full combat, 4 health inserts

Waking Up

  • Closest Liege must move to True Born
  • Spend action in contact to wake
  • Flip to Awake side, insert #1

Forced Awakening (at CLEANSE)

  • Flip to Awake, insert #2
  • Place Fear Counter
  • Targets closest (friend or foe)
  • Gains 2 equipment tokens

Attacking Somnambulant

Don't apply damage - roll Knowledge Die instead:

  • 1: Reality Shift (scatter twice)
  • 2: Matter Mirror (reflect attack)
  • 3: No effect
  • 4: Instinctive Reflexes (moves & attacks)
  • 5: Scryer Overload (1 damage, knock all prone)
  • 6: Reboot (2 damage, summon 3 First Born)
