Understanding Item Card Symbols

Item cards in Core Space use a visual symbol system to quickly convey weapon stats, abilities, and special effects. This guide explains each symbol you'll find on item tokens.

Token Border Colors:

Blue = Weapons
Yellow = Armor
Orange = Equipment
Green = First Born Tech
Brown = Mission Items

Range & Attack Values

Combat Stats
Numbers inside shaped bubbles indicate how many dice you roll at different ranges or in different attack types.
Short Range
Dice rolled for targets 0-5" away. Cloud-shaped bubble.
Medium Range
Dice rolled for targets 5-13" away. Speech bubble with small tail.
Long Range
Dice rolled for targets 13"+ away. Speech bubble with long tail.
Heavy Attack
Dice for heavy/power attacks. Star/burst shape. Uses extra action.
Throw
Dice for throwing the item. Arrow-shaped bubble pointing down.
Close Combat
Dice for base-to-base melee attacks. Circular/rounded shape.

Combat Ability Icons

Weapons
Reliable (Single Gear)
Re-roll ONE missed die. Shown as a gear/cog icon.
Super Reliable (Double Gear)
Re-roll ALL missed dice. Shown as two overlapping gears.
Burst (Flame/Bullet)
Fire additional shots. Burst 1 = +1 shot, Burst 2 = +2 shots. Uses extra ammo.
Full Charge (Lightning)
Energy weapon - no ammo required. Never needs reloading.
Silent (Crossed Speaker)
Does not add peg to hostility tracker when fired.
Target Lock (Crosshair)
Lock onto target for attack bonus. Used with targeting devices.
Ammo Returns (Curved Arrow)
Ammunition returns after use - doesn't consume ammo pegs.
Infinite (‚àû Symbol)
Never runs out of ammunition. Unlimited shots.
Grenade Launcher
Can launch grenades. Shows range (s/m/l) for grenade attacks.
Re-roll Ranged (Target Circle)
Re-roll dice on ranged attacks only. Circular target icon.

Defense & Armor Symbols

Armor
Physical Armour (Solid Shield)
Reduces incoming damage. Permanent protection - doesn't deplete.
Energy Shield (Double Line Shield)
Absorbs damage but overloads if any damage gets through. Must recharge.
Ignore Armour (Shield + Slash)
Attack bypasses physical armour completely.
Ignore Shield (Blue Shield + Slash)
Attack bypasses energy shields completely.

Equipment & Action Symbols

Equipment
Effortless (Checkmark)
Use as a free effortless action. One effortless action per turn.
Flip (Two-Way Arrow)
Token can be flipped to show different ability/state. Rotating item.
Discard (Trash Can)
Single use item. Discard after using.
Repair (Wrench)
Repair damaged items or heal mechanical characters.
Search (Magnifying Glass)
Enhanced search ability. May also appear as "SEARCH" text label.
Swap (Exchange Icon)
Swap items with another trader at any distance (walkie talkie).
Heal (Cross + Number)
Restore health points. Number shows amount healed.
Ammo (Ammo Box)
Reload weapon. Restores ammo pegs to weapons.
Scramble (Movement Arrows)
Special movement. Numbers show squares moved. Jump packs use this.

Explosive Symbols

Explosives
Grenade Damage (Burst Shape)
Center damage value. Three numbers show: center / adjacent / 2 squares.
Mine (Disc Shape)
Placed explosive. Detonates when enemy moves adjacent.
Freeze (Diamond/Snowflake)
Freezes targets. Number shows range and/or effect value.

First Born Technology

Alien Tech
Dyson Energy (Dashed Circle)
Dyson energy value. Used for First Born tech attacks.
Dyson Throw (Fireball)
Thrown Dyson energy attack. Ranged First Born assault.
Dyson Range (Circle + Arrow)
Dyson energy ranged attack. Projects energy at distance.
Phase (Ghost Shape)
Phase through walls/obstacles. Ignores terrain for movement.
Scatter (Teleport Circle)
Teleport/scatter to new location. Random or controlled displacement.
Boomerang (Return Path)
Thrown item returns to user. Doesn't consume the item.
First Born (Alien Face)
First Born technology indicator. Item has alien origin/properties.
Deflect (Bouncing Lines)
Deflect incoming attacks back at attacker or redirect.
Fade to Black (Monitor + Figure)
Become invisible/hidden. Enemies cannot target you.
Infra Lens (Eye Icon)
See through obstacles. Ignore cover for targeting.
Blast (Explosion Star)
Area effect blast. Damages all models in range.
Auto Close Hit (Fist)
Automatic hit in close combat. No roll needed.

Crystal Requirements

Crafting
Some items require crystals to craft. Crystals are found on the board and each type has different rarity.
Green Crystal
Common crystal. Most frequently found. Basic crafting material.
Orange Crystal
Uncommon crystal. Moderately rare. Used for weapon upgrades.
Purple Crystal
Rare crystal. Hard to find. Required for advanced First Born tech.
