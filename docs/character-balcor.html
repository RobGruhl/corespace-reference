Active skill

Active skill

Endurance Skills

Walk It Off
Active + Reaction
Level 1: Restore 1 Health

Onslaught
Reaction + Active
Level 1 (Reaction): After scoring hits in close assault, make another Close Assault action
Level 2 (Reaction): After scoring hits with any attack, make Move (if you wish), then Close Assault action

Level 3: Restore 1 Health (may exceed starting level for this game). Cannot take damage, be Knocked Back, or lose actions/turns until end of next round
